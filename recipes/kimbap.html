Kimbap

Kimbap Recipe Description:

This is a recipe for a popular Korean food called "kimbap." This quick and easy recipe is delicious, nutritious, and can serve as a great snack for all occasions. Even better, the recipe ingredients can be easily substituted to accomodate all your dietary needs!

Ingredients you will need for this recipe:

  • Nori Seaweed
  • Marinated Bulgogi (optional)
  • Rice
  • Cucumbers
  • Carrots
  • Danmuji (yellow pickled raddish)
  • Egg
  • Braised burdock root
  • Sesame oil (optional)
  • Seasame seeds for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Rince your rice under cold running water.
  2. Cook desired amount of rice.
  3. While the rice is cooking, cut your cucumber(s) in half lengthwise and cut the the halves in half again until you have long strips of cucumber.
  4. When the rice is done cooking, set it aside and fry your egg(s) in a skillet.
  5. When the eggs are done frying, cut them into long strips, the same length as your cucumber strips and set aside.
  6. Burdock root and danmuji are typically bought pre-cut however, in the event that they aren't cut the burdock root and Danmuji into long strips that are the same length as your cucumber strips and eggs.
  7. Cook your marinated bulgogi.
  8. Once the bulgogi is done cooking, lay your nori seaweed flat on a bamboo kimbap rolling mat.
  9. Place desired portion of rice on the nori seaweed.
  10. Now for the fun part, arrange the desired amount of fillings on top of the rice.
  11. Lightly wet the ends of the seaweed (optional but helps to seal the roll).
  12. Roll the bamboo mat until the seaweed and its contents becomes a roll.
  13. Lightly brush the roll with sesame oil (optional).
  14. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top as garnish (optional).
