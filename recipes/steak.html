Steak Recipe

Steak Recipe Description

Enjoy this savory protein packed tomahawk steak! Great for outdoor grilling ethusiasts, picnics, holidays, birthday parties, or if you're not into outdoor activities you can even make this in the comfort of your own home!

Steak Recipe Ingredients:

  • Tomahawk steak
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cloves of garlic
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Butter
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Rosemary (optional)
  • Thyme (optional)

Steps:

  1. Season tomahawk steak with salt and pepper
  2. Heat olive oil in a large cast iron skillet (skip this step if you're using a grill)
  3. While the oil is heating peel and crush the garlic cloves
  4. When oil is hot add your steak to the cast iron skillet
  5. Sear the steak for 2 minutes on each side
  6. Add butter, crushed garlic, rosemary stems, and thyme
  7. Turn the steak on its side where the fat is and sear for 1 minute on each side
  8. Cook the steak to your preference
  9. In the last minute of cooking sprinkle red pepper flakes for extra heat
