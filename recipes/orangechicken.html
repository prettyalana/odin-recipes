Orange Chicken Recipe

Orange Chicken Recipe Description

This delicious orange chicken recipe is guaranteed to make your neighbors want to eat dinner at your house everyday! Packed with rich flavors ranging from tangy orange to spicy red chili peppers, you will never have to worry about bland flavorless meals ever again.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Eggs
  • Gluten-free flour
  • Orange(s)
  • Fresh red chili peppers
  • Red chili flakes
  • Fresh ginger
  • Fresh garlic cloves
  • Extra virigin olive oil
  • sesame seeds (optional)

Steps:

  1. Peel oranges (do not throw the peels away).
  2. Using a orange squeezing tool, squeeze the orange leaving only the juice left.
  3. Crack eggs and in a small mixing bowl whisk eggs until fully mixed.
  4. Mince garlic and ginger.
  5. Zest orange peels.
  6. Finely chop red chili peppers.
  7. Season chicken with salt and pepper and set aside.
  8. In a mixing bowl combine the minced garlic and ginger, orange zest, beaten eggs, chopped chili peppers, and chicken.
  9. Mix together until chicken is fully coated.
  10. Once chicken is fully coated, dip the chicken in flour until coated.
  11. Heat the olive oil in a skillet.
  12. Add your chicken and cook until chicken has a golden brown or the inside temperature is 165 degrees Fahrenheit.

      13. To make the sauce:

    1. Combine orange juice, garlic, ginger, and flour in a mixing bowl and mix well.
    2. Transfer to a small saucepan and heat on low heat.
    3. Stir frequently to prevent scorching.
    4. Let the sauce sit for 5 minutes or until cool.
    5. Combine the sauce with the chicken and mix well.
    6. Sprinkle red chili flakes and sesame seeds for garnish (optional).
